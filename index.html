About Us

Established Through the Back to God Crusade

"I will build my church, and the gates of hell shall not prevail against it." - Matthew 16:18

Rev. Nicholas Bhengu, Founding Father

Assemblies of God Church Zimbabwe was established in 1959 under the powerful Back to God crusade led by the late Rev. Nicholas Bhengu from South Africa. For over 60 years, we have been spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ across Zimbabwe, transforming lives through the power of the Holy Spirit.
