Our History

Born from the Back to God Crusade Movement

"The gospel must be preached to every creature. Go ye therefore and make disciples of all nations." - The Great Commission

Rev. Nicholas Bhengu, 1959

Assemblies of God Church Zimbabwe was established in 1959 during the powerful Back to God crusade led by the late Rev. Nicholas Bhengu from South Africa. This revival movement transformed countless lives and planted churches across Zimbabwe. Today, we continue this legacy of evangelism, discipleship, and community transformation through the power of the Holy Spirit.
